Lab Tests

Lab test uses samples of your
blood, urine or body tissue to see
if your results fall within the
normal range.

Radiology Test

Radiology is a branch of medicine
that uses imaging technology
such as X-Ray, Ultrasound to
diagnose and treat disease.

Other Diagnostic Tests

Diagnostic procedure is an
examination to identify an
individual's specific areas of
weakness in order to determine a
disease using external technology
like CAT Scan, MRI, ECG etc.
